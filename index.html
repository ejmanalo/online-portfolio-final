GET TO KNOW ME

Hello there! I am Elaine Joyce Manalo, a third-year student pursuing Bachelor of Science in Information Technology from the school of Dalubhasa, Dalubhasaan ng Lungsod ng Lucena. Honestly speaking, I'm still in the point of figuring out my life would be.

My journey in the world of IT is quite overwhelming since I don't have enough knowledge about IT or skills in coding, I just took it since I'm just fascinated how technology works. However, It excites me when we are challenge to do our websites since I found immense joy in designing or editing something even in my younger years. It is quite challenging but I found joy when I am able to achieve the final output of my project. That's when I thought to myself that making aesthetic design captures my liking.

As I delved deeper into the world of web development, it shows me how creative the technology is. My journey is one of continuous exploration and endless opportunities for growth. It taught me to transform raw ideas into aesthetically pleasing and functional websites. Now, I am excited to see where my journey in web development will take me. Let's connect our minds into making explicit ideas!
